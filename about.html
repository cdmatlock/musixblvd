About MusixBlvd

MusixBlvd is a music creator platform that allows artists to connect social accounts and receive analytics and AI-powered growth insights.

Built by CODEWORK, MusixBlvd helps musicians, producers, songwriters, DJs, engineers, and creators grow their audience using data, insights, and AI-powered recommendations.

What We Do

MusixBlvd gives artists a central place to manage their music presence, track performance, connect streaming and social platforms, and receive personalized guidance from MIKEY AI.

MIKEY AI

MIKEY is MusixBlvd's AI assistant designed to help creators understand performance trends, improve audience engagement, develop content ideas, and discover growth opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make artist growth tools accessible to everyone by combining analytics, AI insights, and music discovery features into one platform.
